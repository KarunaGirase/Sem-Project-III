A Machine Learning Based Support System for Predictive Agriculture and Resource Optimization
1. Introduction
Agriculture is the backbone of the Indian economy and supports the livelihood of a large population. Despite its importance, traditional agricultural practices still depend largely on farmers’ experience, intuition, and historical knowledge. These practices often fail to handle modern challenges such as climate change, unpredictable rainfall, soil degradation, and inefficient use of fertilizers and water resources.
With the rapid advancement of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, agriculture has entered a new phase known as smart or precision agriculture. Machine Learning algorithms have the capability to analyze large volumes of agricultural data and extract meaningful patterns that can assist farmers in decision-making. By using soil parameters, weather data, and historical yield records, ML-based systems can predict crop yield, recommend suitable crops, and optimize resource utilization.
This project focuses on the development of a Machine Learning Based Support System for Predictive Agriculture and Resource Optimization. The system is designed to assist farmers by providing data-driven recommendations for crop selection, fertilizer usage, and yield prediction, especially for drought-prone regions like Dhule district of Maharashtra.

2. Problem Statement
Agricultural productivity is highly influenced by soil fertility, climatic conditions, and effective resource management. Farmers often lack access to scientific tools that can analyze these factors collectively. As a result, they rely on traditional methods which may lead to poor crop selection, excessive or insufficient fertilizer usage, and uncertain crop yield.
The absence of an integrated, intelligent agricultural decision support system creates challenges such as low productivity, economic losses, and environmental degradation. Therefore, there is a strong need for a Machine Learning based system that can analyze agricultural data and provide accurate, reliable, and timely recommendations to farmers.

5. Literature Survey
Several studies have demonstrated the effectiveness of Machine Learning techniques in agriculture. Researchers have used algorithms such as Random Forest, Support Vector Machine, Naïve Bayes, and Linear Regression for crop recommendation, yield prediction, and fertilizer management.
Recent literature highlights that Random Forest provides high accuracy in crop recommendation tasks due to its ability to handle non-linear relationships and noisy data. Linear Regression is widely used for yield prediction because of its simplicity and interpretability. These studies confirm that ML-based agricultural systems outperform traditional statistical and rule-based approaches.

6. Existing System
The existing agricultural systems are mainly based on:
•	Traditional farming knowledge and experience.
•	Manual soil testing and generalized recommendations.
•	Rule-based advisory systems.
•	Limited use of historical and real-time data.
These systems lack predictive capabilities and do not provide integrated solutions for crop, fertilizer, and yield management.

7. Limitations of Existing System
The limitations of existing systems include:
•	Lack of data-driven decision-making.
•	No integration of crop recommendation, yield prediction, and fertilizer optimization.
•	Poor adaptability to climatic changes.
•	Inefficient use of resources such as fertilizers and water.
•	Limited accessibility for small and marginal farmers.

8. Proposed System
The proposed system is an integrated Machine Learning based support system that combines crop recommendation, fertilizer recommendation, and crop yield prediction into a single framework. The system uses soil parameters such as Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K), soil pH, rainfall, temperature, and humidity as input features.
Random Forest algorithm is used for crop and fertilizer recommendation, while Linear Regression is used for crop yield prediction. A Flask-based web interface allows users to enter input values and receive predictions and recommendations.
